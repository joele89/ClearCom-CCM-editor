    Selecting this page gives you access to general management functions of the host device (the device the browser is currently pointing at). You will also access Network, Linking and Port configuration pages here. You can navigate between devices in a Link-Group using the device drop-down menu.

  • The Overview page
    This page shows an overview of all the devices in your system. These may be LQ or HelixNet devices. Devices in the upper part of the page are the units in a Link-Group. As devices are added to the group, they appear in this part of the screen. The lower part of the screen shows the Helixnet, SIP accounts, and IVC ports that your intercom device or Link-Group is connected to.

  • The Roles page
    Use this page to select and edit exisiting Roles or create new Roles for all your devices. A Role is a preset configuration that defines your intercom setup. Roles are used to control configuration and reduce installation time.
    Select a specific section identified with for additional help
  • This is the Assignment page
    This is where you will assign audio to Channels. All devices in your system, with associated ports appear in the left margin of this page. If you are working with an LQ device, audio network parameters are also set in this page.

  • This is the Accounts page
    In this page you will generate and maintain accounts to external systems. Once accounts are generated, ports will be available to be assigned to Channels in the usual way in the Assignments page.
